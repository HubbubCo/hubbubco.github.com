Collaborate, Better.

Hubbub is real-time chat and project tools designed for remote teams. It was created for one reason: to help your team collaborate, better.

Features you'll love

Cut through the noise

Hubbub features topics to help organize the conversations in your rooms, reducing confusion and making it easier to review the important pieces of info at a glance. There's also a powerful search engine built right in to help you dive deep into your organization's chat history.

Stay in sync

In addition to chat, Hubbub features a variety of tools to benefit teams, including Goals and Tasks to help keep you organized, live-updating mentions so you can quickly tell if you've got someone's attention, and notifications on desktop and mobile devices so you never miss a beat.

Video when you need it

Sometimes you need to get a face-to-face conversation going. Hubbub makes it as easy as one click to connect with the other members of your team, allowing you to have a quick chat without needing to go someplace else, keeping your conversation flow intact.
